Interesting Facts About the Squirrel

  • Squirrels can live up to 18 years in the wild
  • Most are small and weigh up to 7 pounds
  • The scientific name for this animal is Sciuridae

Interesting Facts About the Dog

  • Dogs live for 10-13 years
  • They can weigh up to 200 pounds
  • The scientific name for this animal is Canidae

Interesting Facts About the Crane

  • There are 15 different species of cranes
  • They weigh 6-13 pounds
  • They are very tall birds with long necks
